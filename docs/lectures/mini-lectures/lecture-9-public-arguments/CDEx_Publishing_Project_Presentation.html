Goals & Deliverables

Year 1 Measurable Outcomes

  • Create a consistent process for publishing work from start to finish
  • Publish 3–5 faculty and staff projects showcasing AddRan College research
  • Support 6–10 smaller student projects for real-world learning
  • Develop clear editing guidelines and make content accessible to all readers
  • Build written guides so the process continues over time

Capacity & Risks

Realistic Constraints and Mitigation Strategies

  • Limited Staff Time: Small team means we can only take on a few projects at once
  • Academic Calendar: Student workers are only available during semesters, not breaks
  • Maintaining Quality: Limiting projects prevents team exhaustion and keeps standards high
  • Other Responsibilities: Team must balance Digital Publishing Initiative with current Center for Digital Expression (CDEx) services
  • Managing Risk: Clear project limits and regular check-ins on team workload
Speaker Notes: Discuss the rationale for scope control. Transparency about limits ensures sustainability.

Recommendations

Actionable Steps for Success

  • Accept Projects Every Three Months: Take on a maximum of 3 large and 4 small projects per quarter
  • Hire Extra Student Help: Bring in additional students during busy production times
  • Use Standard Templates: Create reusable templates to reduce planning time for each project
  • Keep Quality High: Follow consistent editing and accessibility guidelines
  • Check in Regularly: Review team workload every three months and make adjustments
Speaker Notes: Reinforce actionable recommendations. These are practical steps that can be implemented immediately.

Sustainability Framework

Long-Term Vision and Institutional Alignment

  • Keep Documents Updated: Maintain current style guides and process documents with version tracking
  • Yearly AddRan College Check-in: Annual meetings to ensure project aligns with college goals
  • Roxo Partnership Limits: Only work with external partners when team has at least 25% spare capacity
  • Document Everything: Written guides ensure the project continues if team members change
  • Keep Getting Better: Regular team reviews to discuss what's working and what to improve
Speaker Notes: Conclude with sustainability and institutional alignment. This is a long-term initiative built for growth.
