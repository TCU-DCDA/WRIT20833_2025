## Instructor Notes

### Progressive Hands-On Emphasis
- **Weeks 1-2**: 15 min intro → 35 min practice (more instruction)
- **Weeks 3-4**: 10 min intro → 40 min practice (balanced)
- **Weeks 5-7**: 5-10 min check-in → 40-45 min work time (mostly hands-on)

### Troubleshooting Buffer Sessions
- Session 1 (setup), Session 7 (first git workflow), Session 13 (deployment) require extra troubleshooting time
- Project collaboration days provide overflow time for technical issues

### Differentiated Support Strategies
- **Starter templates**: Available for students struggling with HTML/CSS structure
- **Extension challenges**: Advanced students can tackle accessibility features, custom animations
- **Peer support**: Encourage students to help each other during work sessions

### Assessment Philosophy Integration
- Commit history shows process (earned insight over clean code)
- Reports emphasize reflection and learning, not perfection
- Group work prioritizes collaboration and meaningful analysis over technical polish
